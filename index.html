About Anjuman Polytechnic

ANJUMAN POLYTECHNIC HUBLI

Vision: To be a premier technical institution that prepares students for successful careers and contributes to technological advancement and societal development.

Mission: To provide comprehensive technical education through innovative curriculum, experienced faculty, and modern infrastructure, fostering innovation, creativity, and practical skills among our students. Our commitment is to equip graduates with the knowledge and expertise required in today's competitive job market.

ANJUMAN POLYTECHNIC is a leading educational institute accredited by NAAC with government recognition, offering quality polytechnic education since 1998. We specialize in providing hands-on training in various technical fields including Computer Science, Mechanical Engineering, Electrical & Electronics, Civil Engineering, Electronics & Communication, and Automobile Engineering.

With over 28 years of excellence, we have established a strong reputation for producing skilled professionals who are highly sought after by leading industries. Our student-centric approach focuses on practical application of theoretical knowledge through well-equipped laboratories, industry collaborations, and internship opportunities.

We believe in holistic development of our students. Beyond technical education, we encourage participation in extracurricular activities, sports, cultural events, and social initiatives. Our supportive faculty and modern infrastructure create an ideal learning environment for students to excel.
